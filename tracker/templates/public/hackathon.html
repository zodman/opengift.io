What’s an OpenGift hackathon?

Every week we develop a new open source library or solution here. Free to use and available for all. Many customers from around the world collectively fund the new open source project. The winner is selected by voting.

Join us in telegram to stay tuned!

The 4th OpenGift online-hackathon has started!

The following task was voted on by the community:

AR Objects Disappearing

Hello!

What do you think about augmented reality? Is it a future? We think, definitely, yes! We know a lot about AR, for now, right? We have many open source libraries that can help us to create some virtual objects in real life like this: https://github.com/artoolkit . So where is the challenge?

But what if I offer you to force a REAL object to DISAPPEAR on the smartphone screen? No, I’m not talking about complicated objects or surfaces. Let it be, for example, a pencil on the table or an A4 document. But it also might be anything you want! Just turn on your imagination! Don't try to create something big, the tiny prototype might be enough to get the prize!

We offer you to start creating your own open source project with a promising future! It will be the really useful utility for many cases in many industries. We will start to promote the better result and will be attracting financing to its development. But anyway it will be something special and we hope you will have a lot of fun! Let's start!

Current hackathon rules

1. You need to upload the result to the GitHub open repository and publish it as a result of OpenGift hackathon task; you have until 09/19/2018 1 pm.

2. Anything that isn’t mentioned in the requirements are up to you. Please note that you don't need to meet ALL the specified requirements to qualify for the prize!

3. Referees will choose the best 1 developer who will get the prize fund in GIFT tokens. * The winner will be chosen by 3 criteria: code clarity, UX, compliance with the requirements.

4. The author of the best solution will get the 100% ownership of the new software solution on the OpenGift platform. **

We wish you a good luck. Let the best developer win!

** The owners of open-source project in OpenGift receives a 10% of other contributors income from new features development and bug fixes. The owner may give part or full of his/her ownership to any other member of OpenGift community.

*GIFT tokens is an inner currency of OpenGift platform. It can be used to pay or donate the developers. The network of independent interchange points will exchange GIFTs to fiat money but in the context of financial operations with tasks on the OpenGift platform.

How to participate?

  • Register you GIFT wallet here prior to the start of the hackathon.
  • Wait for the hackathon started.
  • Provide your GitHub link for the task when completed.
  • Your reward will be transferred into your wallet after the voting.

Requirements for participants

  • Everyone can take part in the contest.
  • Project work results should be published in a public repository.
  • Project results should have either a public interface or a video that demonstrates the functional solution.
