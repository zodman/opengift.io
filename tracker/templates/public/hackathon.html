The 8th OpenGift online-hackathon has started!

8th OpenGift Hackathon Task:

Web On Fire

Hello, guys!

Today’s task is pretty simple. You should create maximally realistic fire on JS. Paper burning effect on the webpage would be a bonus.

Let’s set this weekend on fire! Good luck!

Current hackathon rules

1. You need to upload your solution to a public repository and publish it as a result on the OpenGift hackathon task page; the deadline 5 of November 2018 1 pm.

2. Anything that isn’t mentioned in the requirements is up to you. Please note that you don't need to meet ALL the specified requirements to qualify for the prize!

3. Referees will choose the best developer, who will get the prize fund in GIFT tokens. * The winner will be chosen by 3 criteria: code clarity, UX, compliance with the requirements.

4. The author of the best solution will get the 100% ownership of the new software solution on the OpenGift platform. **

We wish you a good luck. Let the best developer win!

** Owners of an open-source project on the OpenGift platform receive 10% of all payments (for new features, buf fixes, etc.) towards the project just for having the 'owner' status and up to 95% if they do the actual job. The owner may give away his/her ownership stake (or a part of it) to any other member of the OpenGift community.

*GIFT token is an inner currency of the OpenGift platform. It can be used to pay developers or donate towards open source projects. The network of independent exchange points will buy back GIFTs from developers.

How to participate?

  • Register you GIFT wallet here prior to the start of the hackathon.
  • Wait for the hackathon started.
  • Provide your GitHub link for the task when completed.
  • Your reward will be transferred into your wallet after the voting.

Requirements for participants

  • Everyone can take part in the contest.
  • Project work results should be published in a public repository.
  • Project results should have either a public interface or a video that demonstrates the functional solution.
